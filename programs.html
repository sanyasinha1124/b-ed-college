Our Programs

Explore the academic programs we offer in education

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)
Duration: 2 Years (Full-Time)
Eligibility: Graduate with minimum 50% (45% for reserved)
Curriculum: Educational psychology, pedagogy, internship, ICT in education
Master of Education (M.Ed)
Duration: 2 Years (Full-Time)
Eligibility: B.Ed or equivalent degree with minimum 55%
Curriculum: Research methods, advanced pedagogy, dissertation, leadership in education
Ph.D. in Education
Duration: Minimum 3 Years
Eligibility: M.Ed with 55% or NET/JRF qualified
Curriculum: Doctoral research, thesis work, seminars, advanced theory in education
